Tickets booking

Designing web service for booking plane tickets (premium class)

Development time: 2 days

Background

Vic works in the USA as a travel agent, with premium segment only. He can easily find a ticket for you, that will be cheaper than in any other places. Vic suggests that it will be really helpful if we can build some service with these cheap flights for the premium segment for showing them and decide, to they need online-service or not. However, the businessman will do not use them service themselves, only they secretaries.

My Role

  • Prototyping
  • Wireframing
  • Interviewing

Main Page

We decide that the main page must be easy as they can be: you need to choose several options and search for fares. Simply — choose and click.

Search Results

This is where all work goes: only needed information is displayed: when and from are client flying, and two buttons for the published fare (that you can see at any booking platform) and special price just for Vic clients.

My Bookings

This is were user to see his profit and all tickets: he can use this one to see all his flights, and in addition: the economy of that flights. I think that every passenger, especially businessman, wants to know how much his savings in flying with Vic.

Results

The idea of online service and service itself liked customers Vic. Now we are thinking about how to develop it with fewer expenses. You can find clickable prototype here.
